
Rule: The Prefix in SIDerivedUnit should equal the sum of all prefixes in subunits
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

When multiplying simple units

g * m = <g.m>

kg * km = <kg.km>

kg * m/s^2 = <N>

g * km/s^2 = <N>

kg * km/s^2 = <kN>

Mg * km/s^2 = <MN>

kw * s = kJ
